## RESHADES – Residential Shading by Human Action and Decision Evaluation Simulation

**RESHADES** (Residential Shading by Human Action and Decision Evaluation Simulation) is a submodel of the [HABITOS](../README.md) framework, developed by the [Energy and Materials in Infrastructure and Buildings (EMIB)](https://www.uantwerpen.be/en/research-groups/emib/) research group at the University of Antwerp.  

RESHADES simulates occupant interactions with residential shading devices, with specific attention to behavioural variability. Two submodels are included: RESHADES_shading, which simulates shading interactions only, and RESHADES_RELICS, which integrates solar shading decisions with manual artificial lighting interactions.
For more detailed information on the functioning of the model and its underlying assumptions, see []().
It is implemented in **Python** and is specifically designed to operate **in conjunction with EnergyPlus**, allowing detailed building energy simulations that integrate occupant behavior with building system performance. However, the code is modular and can be adapted to work with other simulation tools if desired.
